- Save all planets that you have a Command Center on
- Register the endproducts you want to produce
- System tells you what you need (and where) for the endProduct. You select if you want to produce it yourself or buy it.
- If you select "buy", you can specify if you want it private, corp, alliance or public.
- Specify the Buffer time you want to have (i.e. 1 week supply)
- Show who needs your endproducts and how much (per week & total right now)
- You can offer someone to supply all or parts of your endproduct on a regular basis
